Know Your Scrap

Learn about the truth behind your every day meal waste and how it impacts the environment.

What is ScrapTracks?

ScrapTracks is a webapp that allows you to record the trash you accumulate from making and eating meals. With our app, you can input the weights of your waste in different categories: Compostables, Recyclables, and Other. By tracking your waste and reading up on the effects of excessive food waste on our environment, we hope you can become more aware of what you eat and throw away on a daily basis.

What Can I Compost?

There are two categories of what you can compost: Greens and Browns.

About Us

We are a group of students from different universities but share the same mission of saving the earth. We noticed that in our dining halls at school, buckets of food are wasted. We want to bring more awareness to people about the importance of reducing food waste. Therefore, we created this platform, Scraps Trackers, where you can log the food waste you have every day and learn its impact on the environment.

Next Steps for ScrapTracks

We want to expand it to record plastic usage and waste. To tailor the app to educate more people from different age groups, we also want to include free lessons or blogs to share about the impact of food waste and plastic waste from our house's supply chain. We will also have tips on better preparing the precise amount of food and how we can deal with those waste properly.

FUN FACTS

Fruits and vegetables, plus roots and tubers have the highest wastage rates of any food.

Global quantitative food waste per year is roughly 30 per cent for cereals, 40-50 per cent for root crops, fruits, and vegetables, 20 per cent for oilseeds, meat and dairy plus 30 per cent for fish.

At the retail level, large quantities of food are wasted due to quality standards that over-emphasize appearance.
